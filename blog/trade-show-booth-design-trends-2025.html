Trade Show Booth Design Trends 2025

The world of trade show booth design is evolving rapidly, especially in Las Vegas where innovation is key. Here are the top trends shaping exhibition stands for 2025 and beyond.

1. Immersive Experiences

Booths are moving beyond static displays. Expect to see interactive technology, AR/VR integrations, and sensory experiences that fully immerse visitors in your brand story.

2. Sustainable Materials

Eco-friendly materials and modular systems are on the rise. Exhibitors are choosing recycled, reusable, and locally sourced materials to reduce environmental impact and appeal to conscious attendees.

3. Bold Branding and Customization

Personalized graphics, unique layouts, and statement lighting help booths stand out on the busy Las Vegas show floor. Customization is key to creating a memorable impression.

5. Wellness and Comfort Zones

Relaxation areas, greenery, and ergonomic furniture are trending as exhibitors prioritize attendee comfort and well-being.
